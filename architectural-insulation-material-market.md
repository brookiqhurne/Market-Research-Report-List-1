<p><p>The architectural insulation materials market includes various types, each serving specific thermal and acoustic insulation needs. Rock wool, known for its fire resistance and soundproofing qualities, is commonly used in commercial buildings. Glass wool offers lightweight, cost-effective insulation with excellent thermal properties. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) and XPS (Extruded Polystyrene) are rigid foam insulators favored for their moisture resistance and high insulation values. PU (Polyurethane) and PIR (Polyisocyanurate) provide high-performance thermal insulation with reduced thickness. Other materials may include cellulose or fiberglass, catering to diverse applications.</p></p>
