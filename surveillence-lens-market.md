<p><ul><li>C-Mount</li><li>CS-Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Surveillance Lens Market is categorized primarily into C-Mount and CS-Mount types. C-Mount lenses feature a larger flange distance and can accommodate various sensor sizes, making them versatile for older camera systems. CS-Mount lenses are more compact, with a shorter flange distance, designed specifically for cameras with smaller sensors, providing a simpler installation process. Each type serves distinct applications in security and surveillance, influencing factors like image clarity and installation flexibility based on the camera setup involved.</p></p>
