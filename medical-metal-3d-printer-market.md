<p><p>The Medical Metal 3D Printer market encompasses several technologies including Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), Fused Granular Fabrication (FGF), and Laser Metal Fusion (LMF), among others. FDM uses thermoplastic materials for applications like prosthetics, while FGF processes metal granules for producing larger components. LMF, on the other hand, employs lasers to fuse metal powders layer-by-layer, enabling high-precision and complex geometries essential in surgical instruments. Other technologies may include binder jetting and electron beam melting, each catering to unique medical manufacturing needs. </p></p>
