<p><p>The Partial Discharge Detection System market is categorized into online and offline detection methods. Online detection involves continuous monitoring of electrical equipment during operation, allowing for real-time identification of partial discharge activity, which helps in preventing potential failures. Conversely, offline detection occurs when equipment is not in operation. This method typically utilizes specialized testing equipment to assess insulation conditions and detect partial discharges, providing insights for maintenance planning and improving reliability when the asset is not energized.</p></p>
