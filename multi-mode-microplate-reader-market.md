<p><p>The multi-mode microplate reader market offers a variety of detection methods, including absorbance, fluorescence, and luminescence. Absorbance measures the amount of light absorbed by a sample, making it ideal for assays like enzyme activity. Fluorescence involves detecting emitted light from a sample after excitation, commonly used in protein quantification. Luminescence detects light produced by chemical reactions, suitable for assays like ATP detection. These diverse capabilities cater to various applications in research, diagnostics, and drug discovery.</p></p>
